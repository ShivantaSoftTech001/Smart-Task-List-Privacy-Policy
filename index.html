Privacy Policy for Smart Task List

Last Updated: 01 March, 2026

Smart Task List values your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how our app handles user data.

1. Information We Collect

Smart Task List does not collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, or location.

The app may store the following information locally on your device:

  • Tasks you create
  • Task completion status
  • Task reminders and history

This information is stored only on your device and is not transmitted to external servers.

2. How We Use Information

The stored data is used only to provide the core functionality of the app, including:

  • Managing your task list
  • Showing task history
  • Sending reminder notifications

The data is never sold or shared with third parties.

3. Data Storage

All task data is stored locally on your device storage. The developer does not have access to your task data. If you uninstall the app, your stored data may be deleted from the device.

4. Third-Party Services

The app may use trusted third-party services such as:

  • Google Play Services
  • Firebase (if used for analytics or notifications)

These services may collect limited technical information according to their own privacy policies.

5. Children's Privacy

Smart Task List does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

6. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information. Since data is stored locally on the device, users maintain full control of their task data.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be reflected by changing the Last Updated date.
